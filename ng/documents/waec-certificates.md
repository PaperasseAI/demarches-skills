# WAEC / WASSCE and university documents (Nigeria)

For a French study application (the Campus France checklist asks for "copy of the last
diploma/degree — WAEC and/or University certificates and transcripts"), the Nigerian
academic documents involved are:

## WAEC / WASSCE (secondary school)
- **WASSCE** = West African Senior School Certificate Examination, issued by **WAEC**
  (West African Examinations Council). The result is the standard proof of secondary
  education for admissions.
- Two things get confused:
  - the **statement of result / certificate** (the physical/printed document), and
  - the **online result** on **waecdirect.org**, checked with a **result-checker PIN**
    (bought as a scratch card or e-PIN). Each PIN allows a limited number of checks —
    don't exhaust it; institutions may want to verify online themselves.
- Institutions abroad often verify WAEC results directly through WAEC's verification
  service rather than trusting a printout, so keep the exam **year, type (May/June or
  Nov/Dec), centre and candidate number** to hand.

## University documents
- **Certificate/diploma** (degree) and **academic transcript** (courses + grades),
  issued by the awarding university. Transcripts are usually sent by the institution;
  request them early — Nigerian universities can be slow.
- A **NYSC discharge/exemption certificate** may also be requested for graduates,
  depending on the destination requirement.

## Using them in France
Academic documents for a French file generally need to be **authenticated for use
abroad** (see [authenticate-documents](../demarches/authenticate-documents.md)) and to
meet France's requirement for foreign documents — legalisation/apostille + a sworn
French translation unless the institution accepts English (see
[France's foreign-document rules](../../fr/pieces/foreign-documents.md)). Campus France
(via the Études en France portal) is usually the first step for Nigerian students and
states exactly which documents and formats it wants — follow its checklist.

## Pièges connus
- Relying on a WAEC printout when the institution wants an **online/WAEC-verified**
  result — set up the checker PIN and keep exam identifiers.
- Requesting transcripts too late (a common cause of missed intake deadlines).
- Name mismatches across WAEC, university documents, passport and birth certificate.
