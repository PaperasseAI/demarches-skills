# Carte des cadres — 1447-C (CFE, déclaration initiale)

The **1447-C** is the one-time CFE déclaration a newly created company files by
**31 December of its creation year** (see the [CFE guide](../demarches/cfe.md)).
It is a short 4-page form organised in lettered **cadres** (frames), not coded
boxes like the 2042 — so this map is a **section map**.

## Section map (from a filed original; per-box detail contributions welcome)
- **Cadre A1 — Identification de l'entreprise**: dénomination, SIREN, activity,
  address of the principal establishment.
- **Cadre A2 — Adresse où s'exerce l'activité**: where the activity is actually
  carried out. **A company with no premises (domiciliated, founder working from
  home) still files — this is the cadre that carries that address.**
- **Cadre A3 — Origine de l'établissement**: création, transfert, or reprise.
- **Cadre B1 / B2 — L'établissement**: surface, usage of the premises, and the
  elements the cotisation is assessed on.
- **Section C — Exonérations**: tick the exemptions claimed (a first-year company
  with CA ≤ 5 000 € typically ends at 0 € — see the CFE guide). "Néant" when none.
- **Section D — Divers**: remaining cases; "néant" for most young companies.

## Notes
- Filed with the **SIE** (paper or via the espace professionnel's messagerie when
  regularising a missed deadline).
- Sign and keep a copy; the SIE's accusé is the proof of regularisation.
