# Valider son VLS-TS

Arriving in France on a long-stay visa (étudiant, salarié, visiteur, conjoint…)
marked **VLS-TS** ("valant titre de séjour")? The visa only *becomes* your residence
permit once you **validate it online within 3 months of arrival** and pay the tax.
Skip it and you are irregular at month 3: no legal re-entry after travel, problems
with CAF, and a compromised renewal.

## Éligibilité / Qui est concerné
- Everyone entering with a visa sticker bearing « VLS-TS ». (Visas marked
  « carte de séjour à solliciter » follow a different path — a préfecture
  application, not the online validation.)

## Étapes
1. Arrive in France; keep the passport with entry stamp.
2. Within **3 months**: validate on the ANEF portal (declare arrival date, address,
   pay the taxe de séjour online).
3. Receive the validation confirmation (PDF) — it serves as proof of regular stay
   alongside the visa.
4. Diary the visa's end date: renewal (titre de séjour) starts **2–4 months before
   expiry**, at the préfecture / on ANEF depending on status.

## Pièces justificatives
- Passport (visa page + entry stamp), arrival date, address in France, payment
  card for the timbre fiscal électronique. See `../pieces/pieces-justificatives.md`.

## Délais et échéances
- **3 months from entry** to validate. Renewal window opens 2–4 months before the
  visa expires.

## Portail / Où faire la démarche
- ANEF: administration-etrangers-en-france.interieur.gouv.fr (see
  `../portails/portails-gouv.md` — beware paid look-alike sites; the real portal
  charges only the official tax).

## Pièges connus
- **Le téléservice ANEF bloque ?** (erreur à l'enregistrement, dépôt impossible) —
  causes connues, spec des preuves et échelle d'escalade : [anef-debloquer](anef-debloquer.md).
- The 3-month clock runs from **entry into the Schengen area**, not from settling in.
- Students often conflate CVEC payment / campus registration with VLS-TS validation —
  they are separate obligations.
