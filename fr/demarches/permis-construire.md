# Permis de construire / déclaration préalable

Which authorisation you need depends on surface and zone: roughly, **≤ 20 m²** of new
surface → **déclaration préalable (DP)** (up to 40 m² in zones urbaines of a PLU);
beyond → **permis de construire (PC)**. City-hall (mairie) instructs both.

## Étapes
1. Check the parcel's rules (PLU) at the mairie or on the Géoportail de l'urbanisme.
2. Prepare the dossier: **Cerfa** (PC or DP) + plans (situation, masse, coupes,
   façades, insertion).
3. File at the mairie or online via the commune's **guichet numérique (GNAU)** / the
   AD'AU assistant on service-public.gouv.fr.
4. Instruction: **DP ~1 month, PC ~2–3 months** (site/zone dependent). Silence can be
   tacit acceptance — get the certificat.
5. Display the **panneau d'affichage** on site (starts the 2-month third-party recourse
   window); declare start (DOC) and completion (DAACT).

## Pièces justificatives
- Cerfa + plans; architect **mandatory above 150 m²** of surface de plancher.

## Délais et échéances
- Recourse des tiers: 2 months from continuous display. Authorisation validity:
  3 years (extendable).

## Portail / Où faire la démarche
- Mairie / GNAU of the commune · AD'AU on service-public.gouv.fr. Taxes (taxe
  d'aménagement) come after — simulate on the official simulator.

## Pièges connus
- Building at 19 m² "to avoid papers" but crossing thresholds with existing surface.
- Skipping the display panel — the recourse window never closes.
- Zone ABF (bâtiments de France): longer delays, stricter rules.
