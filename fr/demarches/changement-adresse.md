# Changement d'adresse

One teleservice declares the new address to several organisms at once — and a handful
of actors must be told separately, some with deadlines (carte grise: **1 month**).

## Étapes
1. Use **« Je change de coordonnées »** on service-public.gouv.fr: notifies CAF, CPAM,
   impôts, France Travail, l'Assurance retraite, énergie, etc. in one pass.
2. Update the **carte grise** address (immatriculation.ants.gouv.fr) **within 1 month**
   — free for the first three changes; ~135 € fine risk if skipped.
3. Update separately where needed: banks/insurers, ANEF (foreign residents — the titre
   address must stay current), school, employer, La Poste réexpédition (paid, optional).

## Pièces justificatives
- New justificatif de domicile (see the recency rules per démarche in
  `../pieces/pieces-justificatives.md`); FranceConnect login for the téléservice.

## Délais et échéances
- Carte grise: 1 month. Others: "promptly" — benefits (CAF) recalculate on the new
  address, so early is better.

## Portail / Où faire la démarche
- service-public.gouv.fr (free) · immatriculation.ants.gouv.fr · anef for titres.

## Pièges connus
- Forgetting the carte grise (the only one with a fine attached).
- Non-EU residents not updating ANEF — préfecture mail goes to the old address.
- Paying a "changement d'adresse" aggregator for the free téléservice.
