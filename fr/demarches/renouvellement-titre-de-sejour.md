# Titre de séjour — first card, renewal, change of status

Non-EU nationals stay legal in France through a titre de séjour. Most requests are now
filed **online on ANEF**; some statuses and some préfectures still require an in-person
appointment. File in the **renewal window (2–4 months before expiry)** — late filing can
mean losing work rights and paying the visa-regularisation surcharge.

## Éligibilité / Qui est concerné
- Holders of a VLS-TS or titre reaching expiry (renewal), people switching status
  (e.g. étudiant → salarié), and first-card cases not covered by a VLS-TS.
- EU/EEA/Swiss citizens do not need one.

## Étapes
1. Identify the right card for the situation (étudiant, salarié, vie privée et
   familiale, passeport talent…) — the card determines the pièces and fees.
2. File on **ANEF** (account with numéro étranger) or, where required, book a
   préfecture appointment (see `../portails/prefecture-rdv.json` — appointments are free).
3. Receive the **attestation** (de prolongation d'instruction or de décision favorable)
   — it maintains rights while the card is produced.
4. Pay the **taxes/timbre fiscal** requested at decision, then collect or receive the card.

## Pièces justificatives
- Passport, current titre/VLS-TS, photos agréées or e-photo, justificatif de domicile,
  status-specific proofs (enrolment, work contract, family records…), and the signed
  **CERP** (contrat d'engagement au respect des principes de la République — required
  for every request since 2024, see `../pieces/pieces-justificatives.md`).

## Délais et échéances
- Renewal window: **2 to 4 months before expiry** (ANEF shows the exact opening).
- Filing after expiry may trigger the **droit de visa de régularisation (180 €)**.

## Portail / Où faire la démarche
- **ANEF**: administration-etrangers-en-france.interieur.gouv.fr. Fees are paid as
  **timbres fiscaux** (timbres.impots.gouv.fr) — amounts depend on the card; confirm on
  the decision notice or service-public.gouv.fr.

## Pièges connus
- **Le téléservice ANEF bloque ?** (erreur à l'enregistrement, dépôt impossible) —
  causes connues, spec des preuves et échelle d'escalade : [anef-debloquer](anef-debloquer.md).
- Waiting for a préfecture email that never comes — track the ANEF espace messages.
- Paying a "rendez-vous" site: préfecture slots are **free**, only .gouv.fr links count.
- Changing address without updating ANEF — notifications go astray.
