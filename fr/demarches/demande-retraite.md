# Demande de retraite

The pension is **not automatic** — you claim it, ideally **4 to 6 months before** the
chosen departure date. One online demande covers all régimes (base + complémentaire).

## Éligibilité / Qui est concerné
- From the legal minimum age (born-year dependent — check your relevé); full rate
  depends on trimestres. Foreign careers: EU regulations and bilateral conventions can
  totalise periods — declare every country worked in.

## Étapes
1. Check the **relevé de carrière** on lassuranceretraite.fr / info-retraite.fr and fix
   gaps EARLY (missing employers, foreign periods).
2. File the **demande unique de retraite** online (info-retraite.fr routes it to every
   régime, Agirc-Arrco included) 4–6 months ahead.
3. Track the espace; answer pièces requests; receive the **notification de retraite**.

## Pièces justificatives
- RIB, ID, last payslips/unemployment records, livret de famille if relevant, foreign
  career records (work books, affiliation certificates) for totalisation.

## Délais et échéances
- File 4–6 months before departure. Pension starts the 1st of the chosen month —
  never before the demande.

## Portail / Où faire la démarche
- **info-retraite.fr** (inter-régimes) / **lassuranceretraite.fr**. Free.

## Pièges connus
- Discovering career gaps at claim time instead of years before.
- Forgetting foreign periods — they can change the rate substantially.
- Confusing âge légal, taux plein, and complementary rules.
