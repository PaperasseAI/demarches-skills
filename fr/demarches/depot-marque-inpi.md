# Déposer une marque (INPI)

A French trademark is filed online at the INPI, per **classes** of goods/services
(Nice classification). Protection runs **10 years, renewable**, from the filing date.

## Étapes
1. **Search availability first** (INPI base marques + similar signs — identical AND
   confusingly similar): a filing does not cure a conflict.
2. Choose the classes precisely (fees are per class).
3. File on **inpi.fr** (compte INPI, e-procédure). Base fee ~190 € for one class,
   ~40 € per extra class (electronic filing — confirm current tariffs on inpi.fr).
4. Publication in the **BOPI** opens a **2-month opposition window** for prior-rights
   holders; INPI also examines absolute grounds.
5. Registration certificate (~5 months without opposition); diary the **10-year
   renewal**.

## Pièces justificatives
- The sign (word/logo file), the classes list, applicant identity; no proof of use at
  filing (but use becomes required to defend after 5 years).

## Délais et échéances
- Opposition: 2 months from BOPI publication. Renewal: every 10 years (grace period
  with surcharge).

## Portail / Où faire la démarche
- **inpi.fr** — only official channel; beware invoice-like scams ("registre" letters
  asking payment after filing: they are not the INPI).

## Pièges connus
- Skipping the similarity search and losing to an opposition.
- Filing in the wrong/too-few classes (no adding classes later — new filing needed).
- Paying fake "European register" invoices that mimic official mail.
