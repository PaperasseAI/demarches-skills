# ANTS / France Titres — identity & vehicle titles online

Grounding for passeport, CNI and carte grise. All run through **France Titres (ANTS)**;
there is **no official paid intermediary** — genuine hosts are `*.ants.gouv.fr`,
`service-public.gouv.fr`, `timbres.impots.gouv.fr`. Buy the timbre at
**timbres.impots.gouv.fr**. The exact pièces depend on the case (first request vs renewal vs
loss/theft, adult vs minor) — confirm the live checklist; below are the common adult cases.

## Passeport

- **Start**: pré-demande on `passeport.ants.gouv.fr` (keep the QR/numéro), then an
  **in-person RDV at a mairie équipée** (biometric station — not every commune, often
  appointment-only) for the dossier + fingerprints, then **collect in person** (within
  **3 months** or it's destroyed).
- **Cost (timbre, métropole, 2026)**: adult **86 €**, minor 15–17 **42 €**, minor < 15 **17 €**
  (higher from abroad; Guyane adult 43 €).
- **Pièces (adult)**: a compliant photo (or e-photo), **justificatif de domicile < 1 an**, the
  timbre number, and proof of identity. With a valid CNI (or expired < 5 yrs) the identity
  check is simple; otherwise an **acte de naissance < 3 mois** (copie intégrale / extrait avec
  filiation) is needed **unless** the birth commune is dématérialisée (COMEDEC), plus a
  justificatif de nationalité if not evident.
- **Validity**: 10 years (adult), 5 years (minor). **Délai**: officially variable ("dépend de
  la période et du lieu") — do not promise a fixed turnaround.

## Carte nationale d'identité (CNI)

- **Start**: same France Titres portal (`passeport.ants.gouv.fr`) and the **same mairie-équipée
  RDV** flow as the passport (fingerprints in person).
- **Cost**: **free** when you present the old card; **25 €** (timbre) when renewing a **lost or
  stolen** card (12,50 € en Guyane). Renewing a valid card to the new format is also free.
- **Pièces (adult renewal after loss)**: compliant photo, **justificatif de domicile < 1 an**,
  a **déclaration de perte** (made on-site, or Cerfa 14011), the 25 € timbre, and an acte de
  naissance < 3 mois only if the birth commune isn't dématérialisée.
- **Validity**: 10 years (cards in the carte-bancaire format since 2021). The 2014–2021 admin
  extension of older adult cards to 15 yrs is real but **not recognised by every country** for
  travel — flag this to a traveller.

## Certificat d'immatriculation (carte grise)

- **100 % online** on `immatriculation.ants.gouv.fr` (FranceConnect login) or the **Simplimmat**
  app — **there are no préfecture counters** for this since 2017.
- **Buying a used vehicle (changement de titulaire)**, request within **1 month**. Pièces: the
  **code de cession** from the seller (the seller declares the cession first), certificat de
  cession **Cerfa 15776**, the old carte grise barrée ("Cédé le …" + signature), a valid
  **contrôle technique** (< 6 mois at sale, vehicles > 4 yrs), **justificatif de domicile
  < 6 mois**, demande **Cerfa 13750**, and an attestation of valid assurance + matching permis.
- **Cost = a sum, not a fixed price**: regional **taxe (cheval fiscal × puissance fiscale,
  set per région)** + any malus + **taxe fixe 11 €** + **redevance d'acheminement 2,76 €**. Use
  the official simulator (`service-public.gouv.fr/simulateur/calcul/cout-certificat-immatriculation`)
  — never quote a single carte grise price.
- You immediately get a **CPI** (certificat provisoire, valid 1 month, France only) to drive;
  the definitive card is mailed.
- **Address change** on the carte grise is **mandatory within 1 month** of moving (free for the
  first 3 changes; ~2,76 € afterwards). Missing it risks a 135 € fine.

## Photo

- Compliant **photo agréée** (< 6 months, 35 × 45 mm, plain non-white background, head bare,
  neutral) from a **photographe habilité / cabine agréée**. The **e-photo code** is the norm for
  the permis flow; for passeport/CNI a physical compliant photo is the standard route. Locate an
  agréé photographer via the ANTS geolocation tool. See `../pieces/pieces-justificatives.md` for the norm.

## Common pitfalls

- **Paid look-alike sites** ("carte-grise.org", "passeport-service", "demande-cni"…) that mimic
  ANTS and charge — the official routes carry no intermediary fee.
- Photo printed at home, > 6 months old, or on a white background → systematic rejection.
- Buying the **wrong timbre amount/category**, or buying it too early (valid 1 year).
- Passport: assuming any mairie can take it (only équipées, often on appointment); not collecting
  within 3 months.
- Carte grise: not getting the **code de cession**, missing the **1-month** deadline, or expecting
  a préfecture counter (there is none).
